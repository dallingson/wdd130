History

Jimmy "The Raft" Buffalo founded Really Cool Rafting back in 2006 in order to provide people with opportunities for adventure. He has always been passionate about rafting ever since his early years on the waters. It was his families favorite summer activity, and he wants others to have the opportunities he has had.

It has taken years for Jimmy to build this company. He has developed a great team that is dedicated to giving you the adventure of a lifetime! Check out some of the trips you could go on if you book with Really Cool Rafting. We are here to help you have fun! We hope you enjoy your experience with us!
